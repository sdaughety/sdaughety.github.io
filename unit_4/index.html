Deviled Eggs

Ingredients

  • 8 large peeled hard boiled eggs
  • 1/3 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon white-wine vinegar
  • 1/4 teaspoon hot sauce
  • Kosher salt and fresh ground pepper
  • Paprika for garnish
  • Optional: 1/4 cup minced red onion

Directions

  1. In a medium bowl, mix mayonnaise, mustard, vinegar, and hot sauce.
  2. Halve the eggs lengthwise.
  3. Remove the yokes and add them to the mayonnaise mixture. Season with salt and pepper. Mash with a fork until smooth.
  4. Mound the yolk mixture into the egg whites.
  5. Sprinkle with paprika.
  6. Provide minced red onion on the side.

Serve and enjoy!

Tips

Boiled eggs peel easiest if eggs are 1 1/2 to 2 weeks old when you boil them.

After mashing the egg yokes into the mayonnaise mixture the mixture won't be completely smooth. Let the mixture stand for 15-30 minutes. The bits of egg yokes will be absorbed. Simply stir and then fill the egg whites either with a spoon or a piping bag.

You can cover the filled egg whites with plastic wrap and refrigerate for up to eight hours. Remove the plastic wrap, sprinkle with paprika, and serve.
